Hello there!

This program is used to master your spelling skills.

"Wordlists" button takes you to the space, where you are free to create, edit or delete your wordlists to practice.

After pressing the "Go" button and choosing one of the wordlists the game starts.
Press "Listen" button and listen to the word pronounced.
Then try to write it down in the special field and press the "Submit" button -- and the result appears in the richTextBox.
Then click "Next" or "Prev" to get another word.

If you are sure you wanna finish the test, please press "Submit all and finish".
Voila! Congrats! Here is your result.

Enjoy!
